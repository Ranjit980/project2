Balen Shah

Early Life

Balen Shah was born to a Maithil ethnic native Madhesi family on 27 April 1990 in Naradevi, Kathmandu. He is the youngest son of Ram Narayan Shah, an Ayurvedic practitioner, and his wife Dhruvadevi Shah. He is currently the Mayor of Kathmandu, Nepal.
